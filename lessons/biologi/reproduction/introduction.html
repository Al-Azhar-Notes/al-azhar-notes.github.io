Reproduction System

Chapter 1

Stop.

Definition

The Reproduction System is the human's effort to preserve its kind.

Male Reproduction System

Tracts

  1. Epididymis: for sperm maturing
  2. Vas Deferens
  3. Urethra
  4. Penis

Glands

  1. Testis: producing spermatozoa
  2. Seminal Vesicle: producing food
  3. Bulbourethral / Cowpery: producing mucus
  4. Prostate: producing alkali (bases) for neutralizing

Female Reproduction System

Tracts

  1. Uterus: growing embryo
  2. Cervix: sperm entry
  3. Vagina: outside

Glands

  1. Ovary: producing ovum

    2. Processes

    1. Ovulation: the release of ovum
    2. Oviduct: fertilization of ovum

Female Hormones

  1. FSH: Follicle Stimulating Hormon
    For controlling menstruation and ovum development
  2. LH: Luteinezing Hormon
    For ovulation
  3. Esterogen
    For thickening uterus wall and menstruation control
  4. Progresteron
    For maintaining pregnancy

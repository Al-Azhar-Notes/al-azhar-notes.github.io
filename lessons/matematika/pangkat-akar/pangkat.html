Bentuk Pangkat dan Akar: Exponent

Exponents

xn = x1 × x2 × ... × xn

Examples

  • 25 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 = 32
  • 57 = 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 × 5 = 78.125

Exponent Operations

Multiplication

ax × by = (a × b)x + y

Division

ax ÷ by = (a ÷ b)x - y

Exponent

axy = ax × y

Zero Exponent

a0 = 1

Examples

  • 50 = 1
  • 70 = 1
  • 1000 = 1
  • 28,670 = 1

One Exponent

a1 = a

Examples

  • 51 = 5
  • 71 = 7
  • 1001 = 100
  • 28,671 = 28,67

Fractional Exponent

x y⁄z = z√(xy)

Examples
